# 🛡️ Knightlee

### Smart Safety-Aware Navigation & Urban Safety Platform



Knightlee is a **safety-first navigation platform** designed to help people travel through cities **safely and confidently**, especially during night travel or in unfamiliar locations.

Unlike traditional navigation apps that optimize routes for **speed**, Knightlee prioritizes **safety** by analyzing environmental data, accident history, and community reports to recommend the **safest route possible**.

---

# 🌍 Project Overview

Urban travelers often move through **poorly lit roads, isolated lanes, unsafe shortcuts, and accident-prone areas** without knowing the risks.

Existing navigation apps mainly focus on:

* Shortest route
* Fastest route

But they rarely provide **safety insights**.

Knightlee solves this by providing:

✔ Safety-scored roads
✔ Safer route recommendations
✔ Real-time safety alerts
✔ Emergency support
✔ Community safety reporting

---

# 🚨 Problem Statement

Daily commuters face safety risks such as:

* Harassment
* Theft
* Traffic accidents
* Dark streets
* Isolated roads
* Delayed emergency help

Groups most affected include:

* Women
* Students
* Tourists
* Night-shift workers
* Elderly travelers

Current navigation tools **do not provide safety awareness**, leaving users vulnerable during travel.

Knightlee fills this gap by providing **safety-aware navigation intelligence**.

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

Knightlee assigns a **Lane Safety Score** to roads and routes based on multiple safety indicators.

Users enter a **start location and destination**, and the system analyzes routes to recommend:

* 🛣 Safest route
* ⚠ Danger zone alerts
* 📍 Black spots along the route

The system also explains **why a route is safer**, helping users make informed travel decisions.

---

# ⭐ Core Features

## 🛣 Lane Safety Score

Each road receives a **Safety Score (1-100)** based on:

* Road lighting
* Accident history
* Population density
* Time-of-day risk
* Community reports

---

## 🧭 Safe Route Planner

Knightlee suggests routes that prioritize **safety over shortcuts**.

Features include:

* Highlighting **dangerous black spots**
* Explaining route safety
* Avoiding unsafe areas

---

## ⚠ Danger Zone Alerts

Users receive **real-time alerts** when approaching:

* Accident-prone roads
* Crime-reported locations
* Dark or isolated areas

---

## 👥 Community Incident Reporting

Users can report unsafe situations such as:

* Harassment
* Theft
* Stray animals
* Poor lighting
* Suspicious activity

Community reports help improve **real-time safety awareness**.

---

* 🚨 SOS emergency button

* 🚓 Nearby police stations
* 🏥 Nearby hospitals
* 🏪 Safe public places

---

# ⚙ System Workflow

```
User enters start and destination
        ↓
Route options fetched using Mapbox API
        ↓
Knightlee Safety Engine analyzes routes
        ↓
Safety Score calculated using:
   - Accident history
   - Lighting conditions
   - Population density
   - Time-of-day risk
   - Community reports
        ↓
Routes ranked by safety level
        ↓
Safest route displayed with alerts
```

During navigation users can:

* Report incidents
* View safety heatmaps
* Find nearby safe locations
* Trigger emergency assistance
